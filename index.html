A short backstory

I graduated in 2018 from Cal Poly San Luis Obispo with a Bachelors Degree in Business Administration. My first job was part of an IT company as a consultant, but I never felt that it was what I wanted to do for the rest of my life.

I began my journey in coding in 2021. I wanted to challenge myself to do something outside of my comfort zone, and coding seemed like the perfect fit, as I have always been fascinated with how it worked. I've been completely hooked and growing more passionate and eager to learn each day.

My goal is to learn all the foundational concepts for Front-End development, and eventually dive into the back-end. Ultimately, I want to challenge myself to learn something new everyday and have consistent growth in my technical skillset.
